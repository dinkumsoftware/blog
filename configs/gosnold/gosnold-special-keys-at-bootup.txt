This is for Dell XPS 13 (model 9380) running Ubuntu 18.04.02 LTS


Hit F12 at boot up to get to bios screen.

control-alt-F1       Back to GUI login
control-alt-F2       End terminal loging
control-alt-F3 to F6 Start terminal login window
